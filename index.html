Natural Disasters

Natural disasters are catastrophic events that occurs frequently, ranging in its influence.

Therefore, we want to help to construct a safer tomorrow with providing some useful information in this website! I hope you learned something ^_^

Common natural disasters:

Earthquakes

One of the most unpredictable and life changing natural disasters are Earthquakes, it occurs frequently even though most of the times the chance of you actually feeling it is relatively low.

Floods

Flooding occurs when sudden water overflows or soaks lands that are normally dry. There are a few places on Earth where people don't have to be concerned with flooding. FLooding varies in its development time and most commonly it occurs when rivers or streams overflow.

Hurricanes, Typhoons, and Cyclones

The three names for tropical cyclones are alike as they draw heat from the ocean's surface and use it to power these horizontal, rotating wind. The difference is just in different areas people use different names.

Tornadoes

Tornadoes are rotating, funnel shaped clouds that stemed from thunderstorms and can reach extraodinary speed.

Volcanic Eruptions

Volcanic eruptions emits hot lava, rock pieces, and nauseous hot gases through an opening on the Earth's surface.

Wildfires

Unplanned and uncontrolled sudden spread of fires that can have a great impact on nearby lands

Droughts

Droughts occurs when there are unusually dry weather for an extended period of time.

Tsunami

Tsunami are typically caused by underwater earthquakes that send surges of water ashore that could cause widespread destruction.
